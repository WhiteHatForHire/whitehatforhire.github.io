FAST, POWERFUL, FREE

Faceshield is an Instagram-like filter you can apply to your photos that blocks face detection online. It adds noise to specific regions of your face such as eyes, nose or jaw line to 'fool' the face detector into thinking there is no face in the photo. The photo is still recognizable, but the face detectors catastrophically fail!
